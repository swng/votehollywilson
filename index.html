Holly’s self interest and community focus are rooted in the desire for a better quality of life for all Genesee county residents.

This starts with clean water and air in a community where every persons potential may be fulfilled; a healthy and fully-restored Genesee county where small businesses thrive; quality education; a restorative justice program; and engaging senior programming,

She advocates for a reimagined public safety system, including violence interrupter funding, stronger neighborhood patrols, and continuously training law enforcement to end blight, gun violence, and the war on drugs.

A New Perspective for District 2

Healthcare is a Human Right

Ensure Genesee County hospitals provide high-quality, affordable care.

Safe Streets

Work with law enforcement to protect Genesee County and be free of gun violence.

Families First

Ensure all families have access to housing and good-paying jobs, including for returning citizens.

Vibrant City

Foster economic development for Flint, the heart of Genesee County.

Community Focus

Listen to all voices. After working for several years as an engineer at General Motors, Holly has since worked for Flint schools, law enforcement, and most recently started a nonprofit.
